Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free?

    Yes, this project is completly free. It's distributed on my YouTube channel.

Can I share this server?

Thanks for your interest in helping me grow. If when you say "share" you understand giving your friends a link to my original video, then yes - I'd appreciate a lot if you would share the original video with people you know. However, redistributing this server and making a video about it, or claiming it's yours is strictly forbidden. If you like this, just share the link to the original video. Thank you!

Are there any credits?

    Your players aren't going to see my name anywhere. If you'd like to add it, I would really appreciate that. If you wouldn't, I understand that, and that's why I didn't added any in-game credits. However, there may be places in the configuration files where a link to my YouTube channel is placed. Don't worry, only you will see that when you open the config.yml files.

How do I know every place where I have to change things?

    Good question! Check the documentation of this project. You'll see a tutorial here, follow it and your server will be ready to use.
